Coalition for Rainforest Nations

What they do:

This charity helps protect rainforests by ensuring that developmenting countries get paid if they show that they have been preventing deforestation and environmental degradation. This helps climate change as deforestation is one of the biggest causes of climate change. They played a big role in securing the agreement on forestry for the Paris Climate Agreement and have helped greatly reduce emissions from deforestation.

About us

Who are we?

Climate Hub is a place to help people looking to find out more about climate change and how they can help reduce the impacts of it. We help to direct you to charities working in the fight against climate change and offer information on current climate issues.

What is climate change?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. These shifts may be naturual but human activities, like burning fossil fuels, have been the main cause since the 1800s.

What can we do to help?

- Visit our donation page to find out more about charities that are trying to tackle climate change and donate.
- Research the effects on climate change here on Climate Hub.
- Sign up for our weekly newslater on our contact page to stay up to date with current climate issues.
- Start making small changes in your daily life to help combat the effects of climate change.
- Share our page to help raise awareness for climate change and direct people to trustworthy charities.

Climate change is causing global temperatures to rise, resulting in more extreme weather patterns across the globe. This is threatening... the stability and security in many people's lives and leaving the environment in horrific conditions. We ask you to do all you can to help in the fight against climate change. Even the smallest of changes can help, like swapping out liquid soap for soap bars or using renewable energy (solar panels). If you can, we please ask you to donate to one of the many charities listed on Climate Hub as even £1 will make a difference against the effects of climate change.
